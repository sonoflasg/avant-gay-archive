**Card NFT** (and its evolutions, like *Card NFT 2* or *Framed Cards*) is another experimental project by **[[Evil Biscuit]] (bis__cut) in the [[Avant Gay]] scene. It leans into card/playing-card aesthetics, often with framed physical counterparts or gallery-style presentation.

### Style and Vibe
- **Aesthetic**: [[Trading-card]] or tarot-like designs mixed with Biscuit’s signature chaotic layering, references, and [[schizocollage]] energy. It bridges digital and physical (many include redeemable framed/physical versions).
- **Approach**: Continues his exploration of pushing generative tools into painterly, referential territory — less pure [[PFP]], more object-like or collectible art pieces.

### Scene Context
It fits Evil Biscuit’s broader practice ([[Drifella]] series, [[Heavy Liquid Graphic]] with [[Parker Ito]]) as a more curated, object-oriented extension. The scene loves these for their blend of trad-art presentation with onchain experimentation — think cards as both NFTs and physical artifacts.

It’s part of the movement’s push beyond standard PFPs into hybrid, gallery-friendly formats while keeping the raw, insider energy.
