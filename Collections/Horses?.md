**Horses?** (sometimes stylized as *Horses*) is a notable early collection in Solana’s [[Avant Gay]] (Gay NFT / Avant NFT) scene, created by [[Tojiba Brand Manager]](part of the [[Tojiba]] CPU Corp collective). It is frequently praised as one of the standout projects that helped define the movement’s playful, absurd, and internet-kitsch aesthetic.

### Key Characteristics
- **Aesthetic**: Crude, pixelated, or surreal horse figures with whimsical, maximalist, or kitschy traits. It leans into lo-fi, meme-like, and absurd internet energy rather than high-polish generation—embodying the scene’s embrace of raw, outsider vibes.
- **Style & Approach**: Part of Tojiba’s broader output (including [[Toji 100]], [[disc buddies]], etc.). Features interactive/web elements in some cases, blurring art, play, and collectibles. It contributed to the [[trait-maxxing]]/[[schizocollage]] wave while maintaining a distinct, humorous horse motif.

### Relevance to Solana’s Avant Gay Movement
[[Parker Ito]] highlighted *Horses?* as “brilliant” and very inspiring upon discovering the Solana scene—positioning it alongside [[Drifella 2]] as a key early influence that spoke to [[post-internet]] sensibilities. It exemplifies the organic, community-driven, anti-polish experimentation that distinguishes Avant Gay from Ethereum’s [[EFAN]]-style fine art or generic PFPs. Tojiba Brand Manager is a recurring figure in the scene with multiple strong projects.

Later derivatives/homages (e.g., [[Horses?2]] by Parker Ito) further embed it in the lore and visual canon.
