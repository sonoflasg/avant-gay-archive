**Little Swag World** by [[Supermetal Bosch]] (supermetalx) is one of the standout, fan-favorite collections in the [[Avant Gay ]]scene. Launched on [[Solana]] ([[Tensor]]/[[Magic Eden]]), it’s known for its **swaggy, AI-generated, 3D-rendered characters** with a playful, maximalist, internet-kitsch energy.

### Style and Creation
- **Aesthetic**: Cute, chaotic, swag-infused figures blending 3D, AI (Stable Diffusion and custom workflows), Photoshopped assets, and hand-drawn elements. Think hyper-stylized, meme-aware, fashion-forward little characters full of personality, accessories, and scene references.
- **Innovation**: Bosch is praised for inventive use of AI + 3D — not just basic generation, but something that feels crafted and unique. [[Parker Ito]] has highlighted it as one of the projects he finds particularly interesting and hard to fully understand (in a good way).
- It builds on his earlier [[Super Metal Mons]] (GAN-generated toy-like characters that also became physical porcelain and a chess-like game).

### Scene Significance
- Represents the **AI-forward, inventive side** of the movement alongside pure [[trait-maxxing]] ([[Drifella]]) or restrained composition (Horses).
- Strong community love for its “swag” vibe, accessibility, and how it captures the fun, referential spirit of Avant Gay without being overly dense or dark.
- Frequently listed in “must-have” or favorite roundups alongside [[Drifella 2]], [[Horses?]], and [[Drilady]].

It’s a perfect example of the scene’s diversity: Bosch uses modern tools to create something joyful and character-driven that still feels raw and scene-native.
