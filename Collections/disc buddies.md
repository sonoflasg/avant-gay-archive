**Disc Buddies** (by [[Tojiba CPU Corp]] / Brand Manager) is one of the **core early collections** from the [[Tojiba]] universe on [[Solana]]. Launched in 2022 with **5,000 unique procedurally generated images**, it’s described as “Software for Toji, Friend for you.”

### Style and Vibe
- **Aesthetic**: Faceless, disc/CD/DVD-themed “buddies” with teenage angst, pent-up energy, and a digital/hardware feel. They have a distinct, slightly melancholic or zoned-out personality that contrasts with the calmer Horses or more chaotic [[Drifella]] works.
- **Generation**: Procedural/layered in the classic Tojiba style — clean yet characterful, with strong community appeal as companion pieces to the broader Tojiba lore (Toji 100, etc.).

### Scene Context
It’s part of Tojiba’s foundational trilogy (alongside Toji 100 and Toji-A) that helped establish the collective’s playful, software-to-hardware experimentation in the early Solana [[Avant Gay]] wave. Collectors love it for the tight community it fostered (active Discord/Twitter) and its role as “friends” in the ecosystem.

While not always cited as the *most* influential like Drifella 2 or Horses, it’s a beloved entry point for Tojiba fans and embodies the scene’s early experimental, buddy-system energy.
