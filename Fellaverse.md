**Fellaverse** (or Fellaworld) is the interconnected **lore universe** and family of projects centered on [[Mifella]] and its derivatives in the Avant Gay scene. It’s the chaotic, self-referential, meme-fueled ecosystem that includes Mifella, [[Drifella]], [[Cuckfella]], [[JoFella]], [[chair]], and more.

### Core Lore & Evolution
- **Mifella** — Started as a messy Solana derivative of Milady (the “degenerate counterpart”). It “died,” sacrificed itself, fused souls (e.g., with [[Dratini]] [[Pokémon]]), and spawned the rest of the family.
- **Drifella** ([[Evil Biscuit]]) — The soul of Mifella in a dead Dratini body. The breakout maximalist collection that defined much of the scene’s [[trait-maxxing]] aesthetic.
- [[Cuckfella]] / [[Cuckcore]] — The more distorted, “abused,” transgressive branch — embracing cuckoldry humor, chaos, and self-deprecation.
- **Chair** — The ultimate endpoint: after dissolution, only an empty chair remains (minimalist absurdity after maximal narrative).
- Other relatives — JoFella, [[Constant Fella]], etc.

The whole thing is tongue-in-cheek, dramatic, and proliferative — characters live, die, fuse, and reincarnate in new collections, driving endless derivatives, traits, and inside jokes.

### Why It’s Important
- **Myth-Making Machine** — Turned simple PFP derivatives into an ongoing soap opera that binds the community.
- **Aesthetic Engine** — Spawned or heavily influenced the dense, referential, chaotic style that defines Drifella, Cuckcore, and much of the scene.
- **Humor & Transgression** — Embraces the “gay,” ironic, gatekeeping, and absurd side of Avant Gay — not for everyone, but core to the punk rebellion vibe.

In short, Fellaverse is the **familial backbone** of a lot of the scene’s lore and creativity — equal parts meme, tragedy, and generative art fuel.
