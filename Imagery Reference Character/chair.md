**"Chair"** is the ultimate punchline and endpoint in the [[Mifella]] / [[Cuckcore]] lore within the [[Avant Gay]] scene — a minimalist, absurd NFT collection representing the complete **dissolution/death** of Mifella.

### Lore Context
Mifella’s story is a wild, self-referential saga of birth, sacrifice, death, fusion, and absurdity:
- Started as a messy, experimental Solana derivative/response to [[Milady]] Maker (the “piss-and-cum stained” degenerate sibling/counterpart).
- Evolved through dramatic events: Mifella “died,” had its soul fused with a dead [[Dratini]] ([[Pokémon]]) to birth Drifella, and ultimately **dissolved completely**, leaving **nothing but a Chair**.

The Chair NFT (often tied to “Death of Mifella 5th Official Cuckcore NFT”) is the final form — a single, empty chair symbolizing total reduction, absence, and the scene’s love of ironic minimalism after maximal chaos. It’s peak Cuckcore energy: self-deprecating, transgressive, and hilariously bleak.

### Cuckcore Connection
Cuckcore (cuckcore.de) is the subset of Mifella derivatives that lean into distorted, “abused,” chaotic, and cuckoldry-themed aesthetics. Chair fits as a culmination of that lineage — turning the once-vibrant Mifella figure into an empty object after layers of narrative destruction. It spawned or coincided with projects like [[Cuckfella]] ($CUCK) and others in the “[[Fellaverse]].”

### Why It Matters in the Scene
- Embodies the movement’s **self-destructive, lore-heavy humor** and willingness to go to absurd extremes.
- Contrasts with the dense traitmaxxing of Drifella or Horses — the ultimate “anti-collection” after maximalism.
- Reinforces the interconnected mythos: Milady → Mifella → [[Drifella]] → Chair (and beyond). It’s inside-baseball gold for collectors who follow the full saga.

In short, **Chair** is Mifella’s final form: nothing left but the furniture after the character’s spectacular, meme-fueled demise. It’s both a joke and a conceptual full-circle moment in Avant Gay lore.
