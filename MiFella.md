**Mifella** (and its evolutions/derivatives) is one of the **foundational projects** that helped spark and define the [[Avant Gay]] / Gay NFT scene on Solana. Launched as an experimental, tongue-in-cheek derivative/response to Ethereum’s [[Milady]] Maker (Remilia Corp, 2021), it acted as a bridge that transplanted Milady’s cult-like, edgy, ironic energy onto Solana while pushing it into raw, aesthetic experimentation.

### Core Concept and Lore
- **“About Milady” but different**: While Milady is often described as social performance/cult behavior, Mifella is explicitly an **exercise in experimental aesthetics**. It’s portrayed as a degenerate, “piss-and-cum stained trouser-wearing” counterpart or sibling to Milady — messy, chaotic, and unpolished.
- **Epic, self-destructive lore**: Mifella has “died,” sacrificed itself, had its soul fused with a dead [[Pokémon]] ([[Dratini]]) to birth [[Drifella]], dissolved into a [[chair]], and more. This narrative of birth/death/fusion/proliferation became central to the scene’s myth-making and self-referential humor.

### Aesthetic and Influence
- [[Trait-maxxing]] pioneer: Bold use of digital layers to disrupt/obscure the central character, stacking overlays to absurdity. It helped establish the scene’s love of chaotic, post-ironic collages that feel like wreckage or deliberate mess — a key fork toward what became [[schizocollage]] and the full Avant Gay aesthetic.
- **Derivatives and ripple effects**: Spawned (or directly inspired) [[Drifella]] ([[Evil Biscuit]]), [[Cuckfella]], and many others. It’s credited with detonating the field and giving rise to the experimental Solana wave. Without Mifella, the scene might have stayed dormant or “tasteful.”

### Scene Significance
Mifella embodies the movement’s punk rebellion: irreverent, transgressive, community-driven, and unafraid of mess. It took Milady’s countercultural DNA, supercharged it on Solana’s speed/low fees, and turned it into a laboratory for aesthetics, lore, and inside jokes that still echo across Horses, [[Drifella]], [[Drilady]], and beyond.

It’s messy by design — never neat or heir-like, but the wreckage that crawled out and fertilized the whole ecosystem. A true origin story piece for anyone diving into Avant Gay.
