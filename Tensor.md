**Tensor** (tensor.trade) is the leading NFT marketplace on [[Solana]] and the primary trading hub for the vast majority of [[Avant Gay]] / Gay NFT collections. It’s where most of the scene’s liquidity, volume, and daily activity happens.

### Key Features
- Fast, low-fee trading optimized for Solana’s speed.
- Deep liquidity for scene projects.
- Tools for bidding, sniping, analytics, and portfolio management.
- Popular for both big drops and secondary trading of core collections.

### Role in the Avant Gay Scene
Almost every major project we’ve discussed trades primarily (or heavily) on Tensor:
- Drifella (2 & III)
- Little Swag World
- Drilady
- Horses?2 / Collection 2 for Solana
- MiFella derivatives
- And many more (Tojiba projects, etc.)

It’s the de facto home for the community — fast price discovery, easy discovery of new drops, and the place where scene traders and collectors congregate.

While VVV.so is the launchpad for new mints, **Tensor is the secondary market engine** that keeps the ecosystem alive.
