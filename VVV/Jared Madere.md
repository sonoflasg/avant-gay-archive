**Jared Madere** is a contemporary American artist, curator, and key architect of the [[Avant Gay]] / Solana NFT scene. Born in 1986, he lives and works between New York and other locations, with a practice spanning large-scale installations, sculpture, opera, digital art, and community-building.

### Artistic Background
- **Early Career**: Known for immersive, material-heavy installations using everyday or “tacky” objects (salt, flowers, foodstuffs, tarps, etc.) that explore society, economics, emotion, and spectacle. He has shown at major institutions including the [[Whitney Museum]] of American Art** (2015 solo exhibition), [[Le Magasin ]](Grenoble), [[La Panacée]] (Montpellier), [[Watermill Center]], and international biennials.
- **Curatorial Work**: Founded artist-run spaces like [[Bed-Stuy Love Affair]] (NYC) and [[Mother Culture]] (LA). These were platforms for emerging artists outside traditional gallery systems.

### Role in the Avant Gay Scene
Madere is a central figure who helped professionalize and expand the scene:
- **Co-founder of Galerie Yeche Lange** (with collaborators like [[Milo Conroy]], [[Wretched Worm]], [[Miles Peyton]], etc.). Started as an online/digital gallery and expanded to a physical space in NYC. It champions Avant NFT artists, blending onchain and IRL exhibitions.
- **Founder of VVV.so** (early 2025) — the artist-first Solana NFT launchpad that became the primary minting hub for the scene (Heavy Liquid Graphic, Horses?2, [[Drifella III]], [[Drilady]], etc.). Built “by artists for artists” with extreme customizability.

His work and platforms emphasize psychedelic, experimental, and community-driven aesthetics — blending fine art credibility with the raw, maximalist energy of the Solana Avant movement. He’s often interviewed about the scene’s rise, Yeche Lange vs. VVV’s permissionless culture, and how NFTs fit into broader contemporary art.

In summary, Jared Madere is both a practicing artist with institutional roots and a builder who helped turn the Avant Gay scene into something sustainable and culturally significant — bridging traditional art worlds with blockchain experimentation. 

Jared Madere’s “[[Tents]]” (full title often *Tents* or similar with collaborator [[Pauly Stone Jiva]]) is an **AI-generated NFT collection** (launched ~2024 on Solana/related platforms) featuring **anthropomorphic tents** that fuse demonic and cherubic imagery. They range from backyard-style to grand Bedouin-like structures, blending cute, eerie, psychedelic, and architectural elements into multi-dimensional, intricate visuals.

### Context in Madere’s Practice
Madere trained his own AI models for the project, aligning with his interest in maximalist, spectacle-driven, and materially playful work. It fits his broader oeuvre:
- **Traditional/Installation Work** — Large-scale, immersive pieces using disparate everyday or “tacky” materials (flowers, foodstuffs, tarps, blood, cherries, etc.) to create euphoric, horrific, or abundant environments. Examples include [[Whitney Museum]] installations, RV-based exhibitions, and sculptural operas.
- **NFT/ Digital Shift** — *Tents* represents his deeper dive into generative tools and blockchain, alongside VVV.so infrastructure and Yeche Lange curation. It’s part of his exploration of how digital distribution and iteration can scale personal, fantastical visions.

### Other Notable Works & Projects
- **Early Installations** (e.g., Whitney 2015): Sensory, chaotic environments with rotting elements, pumps, LEDs, and household objects.
- **RV Exhibitions**: Turned a vehicle into a mobile gallery space parked outside the Whitney, hosting multiple artists.
- **Curatorial** — Founder of [[Bed-Stuy Love Affair]] and Mother Culture; co-founder of [[Galerie Yeche Lange]] (physical + onchain gallery championing Avant Gay artists).
- **VVV.so** — The artist-first Solana launchpad he founded, which powers much of the scene (including many of the collections we’ve discussed).

Madere’s overall practice bridges traditional contemporary art (institutional shows) with the raw, community-driven energy of the Solana Avant Gay movement. *Tents* is a great example of him applying AI and NFT formats to his psychedelic, boundary-pushing sensibility.
