**Wretched Worm** (also styled Wretched Wurm) is an artist, writer, and **core co-founder/collaborator** in the [[Avant Gay]] scene, best known for his role with [[Galerie Yeche Lange]] and contributions to the broader Solana experimental NFT ecosystem (including ties to VVV.so).

### Artistic Practice
- **Digital Work**: Creates imagery that feels unusually tactile and physical despite being digital — synthesized wood carvings, semi-gelatinous surfaces, and pieces that emphasize texture and touch. Standout series include **Wanderlust Girls** (a collection of characters) and works like “the way you make me feel is the best part of my life.”
- **Writing**: Produces thoughtful pieces chronicling networked/online experiences, dynamics of the scene, and broader cultural observations.
- **Overall Vibe**: Fits the Avant Gay aesthetic — experimental, referential, and pushing digital tools beyond flat realism into something more visceral and embodied.

### Scene Role
- Co-founder (with [[Jared Madere]] and others) of **Galerie Yeche Lange**, the key curated platform/gallery supporting Avant NFT artists (both digital and physical exhibitions in NYC). The team includes figures like [[Milo Conroy]] [[Miles Peyton]], [[Supermetal Bosch]], etc.
- Helps bridge onchain experimentation with IRL gallery presence, giving the scene institutional visibility while keeping its raw, community-driven energy.
- Involved in the infrastructure side (VVV.so launchpad) that powers many scene drops.

He’s a quiet but essential builder/artist in the movement — someone whose work and curation help sustain the “punk rebellion” while adding depth and tactility.
