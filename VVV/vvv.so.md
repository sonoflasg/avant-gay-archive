**VVV.so** is the **go-to NFT launchpad** for the [[Avant Gay]] / Gay NFT scene on Solana — often called the “DIY minting garage” where the community deploys raw, experimental collections. Launched in early 2025, it’s a permissionless, highly customizable platform built specifically for artists who want full control over their drops without relying on bigger marketplaces.

### Founders & Origins
- [[Jared Madere]] (also tied to Yeche Lange / [[Galerie Yeche Lange]]) is the founder.  
  He’s a contemporary artist ([[Whitney Museum]] alum) and key figure in the scene who pioneered “avant, gay, and normal NFTs.” He created VVV.so to give artists the same bespoke control that Yeche Lange offered internally — fast deployment, custom mint pages, whitelists, coin-gates, even MP4 animation support. The goal: make it dead-simple for anyone in the scene to launch without gatekeeping.
- It spun out of **Galerie Yeche Lange** (the curated gallery arm of the movement), with overlapping team members (including developers like Miles Peyton). VVV is the more open, permissionless counterpart to Yeche’s curated exhibitions — they support each other.

The platform also has its own psychedelic ecosystem elements (character [[Vajunnie]] and token **$VAJ**), giving it a distinct experimental/insider vibe.

### Why It Matters in the Avant Gay Scene
By late 2024/early 2025, the community had largely shifted to VVV.so for launches. It’s tailored for [[trait-maxxing]], [[schizocollage]], and weird generative experiments — exactly what the scene loves. Artists praise it for speed, aesthetic flexibility, and letting them keep the raw energy without corporate polish.

### Notable Collections That Dropped on VVV.so
It has hosted many of the scene’s defining and recent drops:
- [[Heavy Liquid Graphic]] — Parker Ito + Evil Biscuit Halloween collab (dark, liquid, horror-schizo collages).
- **Collection 2 for Solana** — [[Parker Ito]]’s hand-made Photoshop derivative.
- [[Horses?2]] — Parker Ito’s affectionate riff on Tojiba’s Horses.
- **[[Drifella III]]** — [[Evil Biscuit]]’s massive traitmaxxed evolution (one of the highest-volume projects on the platform).
- [[Drilady]] — Parker Ito’s HashLips maximalism piece.
- [[MiFella]] 3 and other [[Fellaverse]] entries.
- Others frequently seen: UNX (Grotto Labs), SUMMON SOMETHING series, NetBash, and a steady stream of smaller experimental drops from scene artists like [[Jim Spindle]], [[Time]], [[Gremlins 2 Official]], etc.

In short, **VVV.so** is the infrastructure backbone of the current Avant Gay wave — artist-owned, scene-first, and the place where the next Drifella, Horses, or Heavy Liquid Graphic-level project is likely to mint. It’s where the punk rebellion actually ships.
