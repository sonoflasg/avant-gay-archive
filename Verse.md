**Verse (verse.works)** is a **curated NFT platform** focused on high-quality digital art and exhibitions, where many standout [[Avant Gay]] / [[Parker Ito]] projects have launched (including The [[Pilgrim’s Living Room Crucifixion]]; Mona Lisa Hyper-Gamble, among others).

### What It Is
- **Curated Marketplace**: Artists and galleries present digital artworks as NFTs, often in themed exhibitions or series. It emphasizes provenance, artist voice, and thoughtful presentation over pure volume drops.
- **Hybrid Approach**: Combines ease of use (onboarding-friendly) with self-custody — NFTs are minted to smart contracts, but the experience is polished for collectors.
- Primarily on [[Ethereum]], but it has hosted or intersected with [[Solana]] Avant projects through artists like Parker Ito who bridge chains.

### Role in the Scene
Verse serves as a more “institutional” or exhibition-oriented venue within the broader Avant ecosystem. It’s where artists like Parker Ito bring ambitious, personal, or larger-scale works (blending his fine-art background with NFT experimentation). Projects there often feel like digital gallery shows rather than standard generative mints — perfect for lore-heavy or conceptually dense pieces.

It complements permissionless platforms like VVV.so (raw launches) by providing a curated, presentation-focused home for the scene’s more refined or narrative-driven output.
