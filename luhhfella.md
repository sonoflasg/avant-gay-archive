Luhhfella (@luhhfella) is a key figure in the "Fella" / rugcore / [[cuckcore]] /  [[Avant Gay]] NFT scene on Solana. This niche blends [[AI-generated]]/hybrid digital art, meme culture, painting influences, and subversive, lowbrow/post-internet aesthetics.

Other Notable Works & Projects

- [[Fellakunst]] — Their ongoing signature series/concept (often described as "alcoholic gay crack folk art" or avant NFT). It includes video works (e.g., Fellakunst shown at Galerie Yeche Lange's "Wendy" exhibition), digital pieces, and planned NFT drops. It's heavily tied to painting-like techniques and lore around "Fella" personas. Previews and physical/digital presentations have appeared in gallery shows.
